Kidwell Investigations

Kidwell Investigations is a Private Investigation Agency licensed by the State of Iowa, through the Commissioner of Public Safety. Jan Kidwell is the sole proprietor and owner of Kidwell Investigations. Kidwell Investigations provide a diverse array of different types of investigation. All reports, communication, and cases are kept confidential.

Jan's experience as a Licensed Private Investigator was gained initially through working under his Dad's Private Investigation Agency, Kidwell's Investigations. Bill Kidwell was Jan's greatest asset of education and experience as an investigator. Bill's life was spent in law enforcement as a police officer, trooper, detective, and special investigator at the local, state, and federal levels. His last 20 years were spent as a licensed Private Investigator. Bill was also the first President and one of the original founders of The Iowa Association of Private Investigators. Jan Kidwell started Kidwell Investigations in April of 2009.

Kidwell Investigations is based out of Iowa City and is currently taking on new clients from anywhere in the United States. Please contact Kidwell Investigations to see if I can assist you with your case.
